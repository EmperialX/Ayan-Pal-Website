About Me

Ayan Pal’ Ayan Pal is a GIC Learning Consultant:
Planning & Execution who Heads the Centre for Advanced Studies at IBM India. 4-time Distinguished Toastmaster and Former District Champion of Table Topics is an award-winning Author, Educator, Volunteer, TEDx Speaker, and AI Enthusiast who has served across Districts 82, 41, and 124. He is currently serving District 124 as a District Parliamentarian.

My Profile
My Story

The recipient of 3 Brandon Hall Awards, and 10 IBM Service Excellence Awards, he has always made it a point to share his knowledge and expertise with others.

Read about my stories.

Ayan is the author of Confessions on an Island—a thriller (Readomania 2016). He has also contributed to several Amazon bestselling anthologies and co-authored Crossed and Knotted which made it into the Limca Book of Records under Literature. He has been awarded by British Council for his contribution in the field of arts & culture and had his work showcased to the Prince of Wales – Prince Charles. He was also listed by Hindustan Times as one of India’s Top 5 Writers (Short Fiction).
